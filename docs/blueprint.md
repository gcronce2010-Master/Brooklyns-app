# **App Name**: CanvasCraft Studio

## Core Features:

- Interactive Drawing Canvas: Provide a responsive and intuitive drawing surface for creative expression.
- Versatile Brush Toolkit: Offer a rich selection of brush types, adjustable sizes, and opacity controls for diverse artistic styles.
- Dynamic Color Palette & Mixer: Enable users to choose from an extensive color palette, mix custom colors, and save their favorite hues.
- Generative Inspiration Tool: An AI tool that suggests art concepts, themes, or starting prompts to kickstart user creativity based on selected keywords or moods.
- Artwork Management: Allow users to save, organize, and load their creations within the application.
- Direct Sharing Options: Facilitate easy export and sharing of finished artworks to various social media platforms or other applications.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Light and vibrant to encourage playful creation.
- Primary color: A bold and creative fuchsia (#E619AF) to evoke artistic passion.
- Background color: A subtle and desaturated light lavender-pink (#F7F1F5) that keeps the focus on the artwork.
- Accent color: A deep, energetic purple (#D026D0) for calls to action and important interface elements, creating visual hierarchy.
- Headline and body font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, providing a precise, contemporary, and artistic feel suitable for a digital art studio.
- Use clear, vectorized icons for tools and actions that are intuitive and easy to understand at a glance, maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic.
- Prioritize the drawing canvas by maximizing its screen real estate, with toolbars and menus subtly placed for easy access without distraction.
- Incorporate smooth, subtle animations for brush selections, color changes, and saving artwork to enhance user experience and provide clear feedback.
